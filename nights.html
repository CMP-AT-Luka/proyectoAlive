The first night on the job,thus the easiest. The only animatronic you should worry much aboust is Bonnie. keep a watch on the left door, if Bonnie is there close it and open it until you cant see his shadow anymore. You'll also need to keep a watch on foxy's pirate cove, in case he comes out and isn't visible on the camera close the left door and open it a few seconds after hearing him bang against the door.

This night will get a bit harder. Bonnie, Chica anf Foxy can come after you. For Bonnie and Foxy keep the same strategy, keep a watch on the left door for Bunnie and in the camera 1-C for Foxy's pirate cove. In this night you'll also need to keep a watch on the right door to check on chica, if she is in the door close it and open it until she isnt visivle on the window. If the door (left or right) dont want to close, it means the animatronic is alredy inside. In case of that situation, you'll need lots of luck for the door to close again or you'll die.
