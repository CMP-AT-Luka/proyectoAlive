Gloden Freddy is considered an ester egg, due to this his aparition isn't very common. There is a pattern you can follow to make him appear in some versions of the game. Avoiding getting killed by him should be quite easy. He will apear as an empty suit sat down in the office after you see the poster in camera 2-B with his face. From there, you have up to 2 or 3 seconds to get the monitor back up. If you dont do this or you take too long his jumpscare will crash the game forcing you to restart it. You can compleatley evade Golden Freddy by just not looking into camera 2-B, but this can be somtimes dificult as you may whant check if Bonnie is there.

There are some ways you colud "spot" Golden Freddy. One of them, and probably the most common, are the alucinations. The face of some animatronics and the frase "its me" will apear several times for a short amount of time. The other way is in the poster of Freddy in camera 2-B. if you enter this camera and the poster has Golden freddy instead of Freddy, when you get the monitor down he will apear in the office.
