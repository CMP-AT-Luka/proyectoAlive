Chica will usualy start moving in night two. To avoid getting killed by her is pretty easy. She will apear on the right door, if you spot her there close the door fastly and wait until you can't see her through the window anymore. If you cant close the door she is alredy in the office, if you have luck the doore will close back again.

The most important places to know how to detect her are on the right door and the 4-B camera. As for the camera, it is quite easy, you will instantly see her if he is there. If she is at the door, you will see her if you turn on the light, she will always appear on the right door. To know if she is gone, you must check with the light. If she is still there, you should be able to see her through the window. If she's not there, she is already gone.
